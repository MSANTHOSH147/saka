🚀 About Saka

Delivering Happiness, One Meal At A Time.

Saka Food Delivery is a modern food-tech startup focused on connecting customers with their favorite restaurants through a fast, reliable, and seamless ordering experience.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Saka began with a simple mission: to make food delivery faster, smarter, and more accessible. We believe great food should be just a few taps away. Our platform connects customers with local restaurants, empowering businesses while delivering convenience to users.
